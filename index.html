about us


what makes our coffee special


Coffee connects people! That’s why we love what we do. Our days are filled with a mission to create smiles, bring warmth and happiness to those who enter our store or consume our products.

We are the first Indonesian specialty coffee shop, woman-owned, in chennai. Our coffee-based drinks use single-origin beans from the different islands of Indonesia.

Dev's coffee beans not only have a unique story behind them but originate from a farm that practices ecologically sustainable coffee production. Those quality beans are then made into delicious signature beverages from recipes created by our very own baristas.

Dev's cafe Coffee encourages creativity and productivity within a team that humbly calls itself the cafe Family.
